Classic Fluffy Pancakes

Light, fluffy, and delicious pancakes that are perfect for breakfast or brunch. These homemade pancakes are easy to make and guaranteed to impress your family with their soft, airy texture and golden-brown exterior.

Prep Time

10 minutes

Cook Time

15 minutes

Servings

4-6 pancakes

Difficulty

Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • Oil for cooking
  • Maple syrup for serving
  • Fresh berries (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in milk, egg, and melted butter. Stir until just combined (lumps are okay).
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium-high heat. Lightly oil the surface.
  4. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, about 2-3 minutes, then flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Transfer to a serving plate and keep warm.
  7. Repeat with remaining batter.
  8. Serve warm with maple syrup and your favorite toppings.

Cooking Tips

Don't overmix the batter—lumps are actually good for fluffy pancakes! For extra fluffiness, separate the egg and fold in the beaten egg white at the end. You can add vanilla extract, chocolate chips, or blueberries to the batter for variation. Keep cooked pancakes warm in a 200°F oven while cooking the rest.
