Chicken Adobo

Savory Filipino Classic Comfort Food

Filipino Chicken Adobo

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 40 minutes | Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken thighs or legs, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar (white or calamansi)
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 tsp whole black peppercorns
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 potatoes, cubed (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot or deep skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Brown the chicken pieces on all sides (about 5-7 minutes). Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, sauté crushed garlic until fragrant (about 1 minute).
  4. Return the browned chicken to the pot.
  5. Pour in soy sauce, vinegar, and water. Stir well to combine.
  6. Add bay leaves, black peppercorns, and brown sugar (if using).
  7. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Add onions and potatoes (if using) and continue cooking for another 10-15 minutes until chicken is tender and potatoes are cooked.
  9. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt as needed.
  10. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Tips

Chicken adobo tastes even better the next day as the flavors develop! Store in the refrigerator and reheat gently before serving. For a richer sauce, you can add a little soy sauce or reduce the sauce longer over low heat. Some prefer adding a touch of butter for extra richness.
