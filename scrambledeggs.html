Perfect Scrambled Eggs

Soft, creamy, and delicious scrambled eggs that are easy to make for breakfast. This simple recipe yields perfectly fluffy scrambled eggs every time with the right technique and minimal ingredients. Perfect for any time of day!

Prep Time

5 minutes

Cook Time

5 minutes

Servings

2 people

Difficulty

Very Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons milk or cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chives or parsley (optional)
  • Pinch of garlic powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk with milk, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium-low heat.
  3. Once butter is melted and foaming, pour in the egg mixture.
  4. Let it sit for a few seconds, then gently push the eggs from the edges to the center.
  5. Continue stirring gently and slowly, about every 10-15 seconds.
  6. Cook for 3-5 minutes until the eggs are soft and creamy (not rubbery).
  7. Remove from heat when eggs still look slightly undercooked—they will continue cooking from residual heat.
  8. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve immediately on toast or with your favorite sides.

Cooking Tips

Low to medium heat is key for creamy scrambled eggs. Avoid high heat as it makes them tough and rubbery. Add cheese, bacon, mushrooms, or tomatoes for extra flavor. Remove the pan from heat when the eggs still look slightly wet—carryover cooking will finish them perfectly. For fluffier eggs, add a tablespoon of cream cheese while stirring.
