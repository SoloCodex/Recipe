Scrambled Eggs

Easy, Creamy & Perfect Breakfast Staple

Perfect Creamy Scrambled Eggs

Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 5 minutes | Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp heavy cream or milk (optional)
  • 1/4 cup diced bell peppers (optional)
  • 1/4 cup diced onions (optional)
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or your preference)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chives or parsley for garnish (optional)
  • Cooked bacon bits (optional)

Instructions

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk together with cream or milk (if using), salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick skillet or frying pan over medium-low heat until fully melted.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the warm skillet.
  4. If adding vegetables, add the diced peppers and onions and let them cook for 1-2 minutes first.
  5. Using a spatula, gently push and fold the eggs from the edges toward the center.
  6. Cook slowly and patiently—scrambled eggs should not be rushed!
  7. When the eggs are almost done but still slightly moist, add the shredded cheese and stir.
  8. Remove from heat when eggs are cooked to your preference (creamy or more firm).
  9. Transfer to a plate and garnish with fresh herbs or bacon bits if desired.
  10. Serve immediately with toast, hash browns, or bacon.

Tips

The key to creamy scrambled eggs is cooking them slowly over medium-low heat and not overcooking. Adding a splash of heavy cream or milk makes them extra creamy and delicious. You can customize with your favorite vegetables, meats, and cheeses. Try mushrooms, tomatoes, spinach, sausage, or smoked salmon!
