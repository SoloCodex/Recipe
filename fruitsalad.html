Filipino-Style Fruit Salad

A refreshing and colorful Filipino fruit salad that combines fresh fruits, cream, and a hint of sweetness. This creamy fruit salad is perfect for parties, gatherings, and hot summer days. It's easy to make and loved by everyone.

Prep Time

20 minutes

Chill Time

1 hour

Servings

6-8 people

Difficulty

Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (20 oz each) fruit cocktail, drained
  • 1 can (15 oz) cream of evaporated milk
  • 1 can (14 oz) condensed milk
  • 2 apples, peeled and diced
  • 2 oranges, segmented and halved
  • 1 can (20 oz) pineapple chunks, drained
  • 1 can (15 oz) mandarin oranges, drained
  • 1 pound grapes, halved
  • Maraschino cherries (optional)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine evaporated milk and condensed milk. Mix well.
  2. Add the drained fruit cocktail to the milk mixture.
  3. Add fresh apples, oranges, pineapple chunks, mandarin oranges, and grapes.
  4. Gently toss all ingredients together until well combined.
  5. Add a pinch of salt to enhance flavors.
  6. Transfer to a serving bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
  7. Top with maraschino cherries if desired.
  8. Serve chilled and enjoy!

Cooking Tips

Prepare the fruit salad a few hours ahead of serving for better flavor blending. You can use any combination of fruits available, such as mangoes, strawberries, or watermelon. For a healthier version, use plain yogurt instead of evaporated and condensed milk. Keep it refrigerated and consume within 2-3 days.
