Fruit Salad

Refreshing & Healthy Filipino-Style Fruit Salad

Filipino-Style Fruit Salad

Prep Time: 20 minutes | Chill Time: 1 hour | Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 can (14 oz) condensed milk
  • 1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
  • 2 cans (15 oz each) fruit cocktail, drained
  • 2 apples, diced
  • 2 oranges, peeled and segmented
  • 1 pineapple, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup seedless grapes
  • 1 mango, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup miniature marshmallows
  • 1/2 cup maraschino cherries
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (optional)
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine condensed milk and evaporated milk. Mix well.
  2. Add the drained fruit cocktail to the milk mixture.
  3. Add all fresh fruits (apples, oranges, pineapple, grapes, and mango).
  4. Squeeze lemon juice over the fruits to prevent browning.
  5. Gently fold in the marshmallows and maraschino cherries.
  6. If using, add the shredded cheddar cheese and mix gently.
  7. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  8. Stir gently before serving and enjoy cold!

Tips

This salad is best served chilled. You can prepare it a day ahead and keep it in the refrigerator. Feel free to add or substitute fruits based on your preference. Some people also add crushed graham crackers or whipped cream on top for extra texture!
