Classic Italian Pasta Dish

Classic Spaghetti

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese for topping
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti and cook until al dente (about 9-10 minutes).
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, basil, and oregano. Stir well.
  5. Simmer the sauce for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Drain the cooked spaghetti and transfer to serving plates.
  8. Pour the tomato sauce over the spaghetti and top with Parmesan cheese.
  9. Garnish with fresh basil and serve immediately.

Tips

For a richer sauce, add a splash of heavy cream or a pinch of sugar to balance the acidity of the tomatoes. You can also add ground beef or Italian sausage for a meat sauce variation!
