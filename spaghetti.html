Filipino-Style Spaghetti

A delicious Filipino twist on the classic Italian pasta dish. This sweet and savory spaghetti is perfect for gatherings and family meals. The combination of ground meat, tomato sauce, and cheese creates an unforgettable flavor.

Prep Time

15 minutes

Cook Time

20 minutes

Servings

4-6 people

Difficulty

Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti pasta
  • 1 pound ground beef or pork
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (28 oz each) tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Water as needed

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat butter over medium-high heat. Saut√© the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the ground meat and cook until browned, breaking it apart as it cooks. Drain excess fat if needed.
  4. Sprinkle flour over the meat and stir well to coat.
  5. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, and brown sugar. Stir until well combined.
  6. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add water if the sauce is too thick.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Combine the cooked spaghetti with the sauce and toss well.
  9. Transfer to a serving dish and sprinkle with grated cheddar cheese.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy!

Cooking Tips

For extra flavor, add a pinch of paprika or Italian seasoning to the sauce. You can also add diced bell peppers and carrots to make it more nutritious. If you prefer a sweeter sauce, add more brown sugar to taste. Leftovers can be refrigerated for up to 3 days.
